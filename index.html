Mount Elaria is a mystical open world nestled between glowing crystal caves, enchanted autumn forests, and ancient Celtic ruins humming with forgotten power. Every path leads to discovery — where mushrooms glow in the dark and sacred stones guard the secrets of a lost civilization.
